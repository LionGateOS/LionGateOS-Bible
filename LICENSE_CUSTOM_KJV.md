# LionGateOS Custom KJV Usage Policy

## Overview

The LionGateOS Custom KJV edition is a custom Bible edition developed by LionGateOS.

It includes documented editorial work, custom changes, formatting decisions, Bible resource integration, and supporting documentation.

## General Use

The LionGateOS Custom KJV edition may be used for reading, study, and approved distribution formats provided by LionGateOS.

Users may use the edition as provided.

## Modification Policy

The LionGateOS Custom KJV edition is not released as an open modification project.

Without permission from LionGateOS, users may not:

- modify the edition
- create derivative editions based on LionGateOS changes
- redistribute altered versions
- represent modified versions as official LionGateOS editions

Requests for corrections, improvements, or changes may be submitted for consideration.

## GrowDaily Use

GrowDaily has permission to use the LionGateOS Custom KJV edition within the application.

Future GrowDaily updates may continue to use and improve the integration of this Bible edition.

## Philadelphia Church of God Use

Philadelphia Church of God may use the LionGateOS Custom KJV edition.

LionGateOS remains available to assist with requested improvements, corrections, additions, or adjustments.

The purpose is to preserve the integrity of the edition while allowing continued development based on practical needs.

## e-Sword Edition

An e-Sword-compatible edition of the LionGateOS Custom KJV is available.

Because this is a custom edition, installation instructions and distribution information are maintained separately.

The edition may require manual installation steps rather than appearing as a standard downloadable e-Sword catalog item.

## Documentation

Additional documentation includes:

- edition history
- textual changes
- source information
- validation records
- export formats
- Bible resource integration
